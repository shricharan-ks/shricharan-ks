## About Me

Data Scientist at **Red Hat** based in Ireland with **5+ years** of experience spanning AI/ML, full-stack engineering, and cloud-native infrastructure. I build end-to-end AI solutions — from model training and LLM pipelines to production-grade inference systems on **OpenShift AI** and **Kubernetes**.

My work sits at the intersection of applied AI research and enterprise engineering: designing agentic AI frameworks, deploying LLMs at scale, and making open-source AI practical for real-world adoption. I've shipped systems that reduced operational costs by **67%**, improved response times by **38%**, and are used by **90+ users daily**.

- Building **Agentic AI** tooling and the **Open GenAI Stack (OGX)** for enterprise deployments on OpenShift
- Deploying knowledge-tuning workflows, model serving (vLLM), and agent orchestration on **RHOAI**
- Previously led a team of 5 at Carelon to deliver "Chat with Medical Document" — an LLM-powered pipeline achieving **89% accuracy** on medical document extraction
- Published researcher — presented at **ISMAC-CVB 2019** on clinical feature selection methods
